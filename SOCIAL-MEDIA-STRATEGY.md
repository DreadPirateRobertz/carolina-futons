# Carolina Futons — Social Media Strategy

## Brand Voice & Aesthetic

**Tone**: Warm, authentic, knowledgeable. Brenda's voice — a trusted friend who knows furniture. Never salesy or corporate.

**Visual Palette**: Blue Ridge Mountain aesthetic
- Sand (#E8D5B7), Espresso (#3A2518), Mountain Blue (#5B8FA8), Coral (#E8845C)
- Natural light, lifestyle shots, mountain-town authenticity
- Consistent warm filter across all platforms

**Core Messages**:
- "Handcrafted comfort at mountain-town prices"
- "Quality furniture since 1991"
- "From our family to yours — Hendersonville, NC"

## Pinterest Strategy

**Why Pinterest**: #1 platform for furniture discovery. Users actively search for room ideas and products. Long content lifespan (pins last months vs hours on other platforms).

### Board Structure
| Board | Content | Pin Frequency |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| **Futon Living Rooms** | Styled futon setups, customer photos | 3-4/week |
| **Small Space Solutions** | Studio/apartment layouts with futons | 2-3/week |
| **Mountain Home Inspiration** | Blue Ridge lifestyle rooms, cabin decor | 2-3/week |
| **Before & After** | Room transformations featuring our products | 1-2/week |
| **Bedroom Makeovers** | Platform beds, murphy beds styled | 2-3/week |
| **Futon Buyer's Guide** | Infographics, comparison charts | 1/week |
| **Shop Our Products** | Product catalog pins (auto from feed) | Auto-sync |
| **Customer Showcase** | UGC repins from customers | 1-2/week |

### Rich Pins Setup
- Product Rich Pins enabled via `get_pinterestProductFeed` endpoint
- Configure in Pinterest Business → Catalogs → Add data source
- Feed URL: `https://www.carolinafutons.com/_functions/pinterestProductFeed`
- Refresh: Daily automatic fetch

### Pin Best Practices
- Vertical images (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- Text overlay with product name + price on lifestyle shots
- Link every pin to product page or blog post
- Use keyword-rich descriptions (SEO for Pinterest search)
- Hashtags: #FutonLiving #SmallSpaceLiving #MurphyBed #PlatformBed #HandcraftedFurniture #NCFurniture

## Instagram Strategy

### Feed Grid
Alternate between three content types for visual variety:
1. **Product shots** (clean, white/natural background)
2. **Lifestyle/room scenes** (styled, warm lighting)
3. **Behind-the-scenes** (showroom, team, mountain views)

### Content Calendar
| Day | Content Type | Example |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Monday | Product spotlight | Feature one product with details |
| Tuesday | Customer photo/story | Repost customer's room with our furniture |
| Wednesday | Behind-the-scenes | Showroom peek, new arrivals, team |
| Thursday | Room inspiration | Styled room setup |
| Friday | Fun/educational | "Did you know?" futon facts, care tips |
| Saturday | Weekend sale/promo | Special offer or bundle deal |

### Reels Strategy (3-4/week)
- **Assembly time-lapses** (30-60s): "Watch this futon go from box to beautiful in 2 minutes"
- **Room transformations**: Before/after reveals with trending audio
- **Futon vs couch**: Side-by-side comparison content
- **Day-in-the-life**: Brenda at the showroom, mountain views from the shop
- **Customer unboxing**: Encourage customers to film and tag

### Stories (Daily)
- New arrival announcements
- Swatch/fabric polls ("Which color for your living room?")
- Q&A stickers about furniture care
- Countdown for sales events
- Behind-the-scenes showroom moments

### Shopping Integration
- Instagram Shop connected via Facebook Commerce catalog
- Feed URL: `https://www.carolinafutons.com/_functions/facebookCatalogFeed`
- Tag products in feed posts and Reels
- Shoppable Stories with product stickers

## TikTok Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Room transformations** — "This empty room became a cozy studio apartment" (before/after)
2. **Assembly content** — "How fast can we set up a murphy bed?" (time-lapse with timer)
3. **Myth busting** — "Futons in 2026 are NOT what you remember from college"
4. **Small business storytelling** — "Day in the life of a family furniture shop in the mountains"
5. **Satisfying content** — Futon folding/unfolding mechanisms, smooth drawer slides

### Posting Frequency
- 3-5 videos/week
- Best times: 7-9am, 12-3pm, 7-11pm EST

### TikTok-Specific Tips
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Use trending sounds when they fit naturally
- Reply to comments with video responses
- Duet/stitch furniture-related trending content
- Use TikTok Pixel for conversion tracking (custom embed via Velo)
