### Week 3-4: First Paid Campaigns

**Google Shopping Ads** — Primary channel, highest intent
- Budget: $30-50/day ($900-1,500/month)
- Start with top 10 best-selling products only
- Target: Western NC + surrounding 200-mile radius first, expand later
- Expected CPC: $0.50-1.50 for furniture keywords
- Expected conversion rate: 1-2% (industry benchmark for new stores)
- Projected: 600-3,000 clicks/month → 6-60 orders → $2,400-$24,000 revenue

**Google Search Ads** — Branded + high-intent
- Budget: $15-25/day ($450-750/month)
- Keywords: "futon [city name]", "buy futon online", "futon frame and mattress"
- Long-tail: "best futon for college dorm", "futon vs sofa bed", "futon mattress queen size"
- Our blog buying guides (cf-cya, 8 categories) serve as landing pages

**Facebook/Instagram** — Retargeting only (not prospecting yet)
- Budget: $10-15/day ($300-450/month)
- Retarget: site visitors who viewed products but didn't buy
- Carousel ads showing viewed products + similar items
- Our Facebook catalog feed endpoint is ready
