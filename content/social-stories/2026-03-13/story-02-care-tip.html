🌿 Brenda's Care Tips

Flip your futon mattress every 3 months.
Just like a regular mattress, futon mattresses compress unevenly with use. A simple quarterly flip — top-to-bottom AND side-to-side — extends the life of your mattress by years.

I've seen customers get 10–15 years out of a good cotton futon mattress just by doing this one thing.
— Brenda, Carolina Futons since 1991
